About us

Rub’n’Duck is a small business that specializes in creation of custom rubber ducks of any kind ! Our store is located in San Francisco and operated by a few different people that we call a family.

Our main goal is to present our clients the opportunity of transforming plain and basic stuff into something colorful and fun, maybe even meaningful !

We can customize rubber ducks into anything you would like it to be — starting from individual portraits and preferred personas to recreating any kind of character of your choice. Just give us a raw idea (or maybe an image what you would like your rubber duck to look like) and we will present it to you in just a few weeks. The size is also customizable. So just have fun with it !
